# Brands Under Shoppers Stop

Shoppers Stop operates under a "house of brands" model. Below is a comprehensive list of their in-house private labels, exclusive global beauty partnerships, and prominent partner brands available at their department stores, SSBeauty outlets, and online platform.

## In-House Private Labels (Owned by Shoppers Stop)
These labels are exclusively developed by Shoppers Stop to cater to specific styles, price points, and demographics. You will find these at Shoppers Stop or their value-fashion offshoot, INTUNE.

* **Stop:** Their flagship private label, focusing on smart, everyday casuals and formal wear for both men and women.
* **Life:** A youth-oriented casual wear brand featuring trendy, everyday clothing, tees, and denim.
* **Kashish:** Premium women's ethnic wear (lehengas, sharara sets, and heavy kurtas) designed for festive occasions and weddings.
* **Haute Curry:** Contemporary fusion ethnic wear for younger women.
* **Fratini (Velorio Fratini):** Sharp formal and semi-formal wear, primarily targeting men's office and evening wear.
* **Bandeya:** Men's Indian ethnic wear, focusing on kurtas, pyjamas, and festive jackets.
* **Alt Life:** Relaxed, printed, and denim-focused casuals for men and women.
* **Elliza Donatein:** Chic western workwear, dresses, and handbags for women.
* **U R You:** A dedicated plus-size fashion brand focused on flattering fits and body positivity.
* **Karrot:** Their primary kids and infants wear brand.
* **Insense:** A line focusing on comfortable intimate wear and loungewear.
* **Arcelia:** Their dedicated in-house beauty, fragrance, and bath brand.
* **Joyology:** A recently launched color cosmetics line aimed specifically at Gen-Z shoppers.
